Child Safety Standards

Last updated: 2025.04.18

At SmashChats, we take the safety of our users seriously — especially minors. Our systems are designed to ensure privacy, security, and responsible communication.

Our Policy Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

We have zero tolerance for any form of child sexual abuse, exploitation, grooming, or harassment. Our app is built on peer-to-peer communication principles with no centralized message storage, but we implement several safeguards:

  • All content is end-to-end encrypted by default.
  • We do not store user messages or media on centralized servers.
  • Users can report abusive behavior directly in-app or by emailing safety@yourdomain.com.
  • We investigate and escalate CSAE reports to relevant authorities.

In-App Reporting

Users can report any safety concerns directly from the app interface. Reports are sent securely to our safety team for review and action.

Compliance With Child Safety Laws

We comply with all relevant child protection laws and cooperate with regional and national authorities. This includes reporting to national hotlines and law enforcement when necessary.

Reporting and Contact

If you have a concern to report, please contact our child safety officer at:

  • Email: safety@yourdomain.com
  • Role: Designated Child Safety Point of Contact

We review all child safety reports promptly and work with appropriate organizations to ensure a safe digital environment.

External Guidelines and References

We follow best practices as outlined by:

  • INHOPE – International Network of Hotlines
  • NCMEC – National Center for Missing & Exploited Children
  • Tech Coalition – Combating Online CSEA

Please be kind. Stay safe. Help us protect our communities.
